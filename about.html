About DSSPL

Dev Sanskriti School Premier League — The premier annual sports tournament of Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya, Haridwar.

Screen Nahi Green

Vision & Objectives

DSSPL (Dev Sanskriti School Premier League) is established with a unique initiative: "Screen Nahi Green". Our goal is to steer youth away from mobile and computer screens towards the active, physical sports field, encouraging healthy team play, strong social bonds, and dynamic athletic development.

By engaging in rigorous inter-mandal matches, students learn the fundamentals of leadership, sportsmanship, and mental resilience. We are building a vibrant, healthy, and screen-free student community.

Physical fitness

Inspiring students to prioritize daily active exercises and sports training.

Team Spirit

Nurturing values of group coordination, empathy, and collective triumph.

Screen-Free Life

Promoting a balanced lifestyle reducing unnecessary digital screen dependance.

Tournament Rules & Guidelines

To ensure decorum, equality, and high sportsmanship, all participants must comply with the following tournament rules:

  • Eligibility: Only registered students of Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya, Haridwar can compete representing their designated Mandal.
  • Conduct: Any form of verbal abuse, aggressive misconduct, or unsportsmanlike behavior on the field will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Scoring:
    • Win: 3 Points
    • Draw: 1 Point
    • Loss: 0 Points
  • Punctuality: Teams must report to their assigned venue (e.g. Main Ground, Vidyapeeth, Shriram ground) at least 15 minutes before the scheduled time. Delay of more than 10 minutes leads to a walkover (win awarded to opponent).

Tournament Administration

The tournament is organized and managed by the Sports Council of Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya, Haridwar. Dedicated committees monitor operations:

  • Organizing Team: Oversees scheduling, score management, referee assignments, and mandal points calculations.
  • Media & Content Team: Manages website articles, highlights, announcement alerts, and news.
  • Gallery & Creative Team: Records match videos, captures premium graphics, and publishes media assets.

The competition involves 7 dedicated Mandals: Adarsh Mandal, Sankalp Mandal, Chanakya Mandal, Vijay Mandal, Utkarsh Mandal, Rakshak Mandal, and Shaurya Mandal.
